Quick Reload



Quick reload is an uncommon card that gives the player -70% reload time. There are no downsides to taking this card making it an easy pick whenever it appears in your selection. This card synergises well with any build and acts as good add-on to an already polished build. However, this card is also more then capable of acing your opponent when used right on its own.
