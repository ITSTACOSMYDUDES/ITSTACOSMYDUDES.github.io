Shields Up



Shields Up is a rare card that triggers a block upon firing the last bullet from your magazine. This comes at the downside of +0.5 seconds of reload time and block cooldown. This card synergises well with any cards that give a block abiliity such as overpower or bombs away or with tactical relod as ths combination will give you infinite ammo due to tactical relod fully reloading your gun upon block.
