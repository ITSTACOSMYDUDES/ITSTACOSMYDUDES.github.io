Static Field



Static Field is an uncommon card that spawns a damaging aura at the location of the player upon blocking. This comes at the downside of +0.25 seconds of block cooldown This card synergises well with card that give extra blocks such as echo or shield charge as this allows the ability to activate multiple times.
