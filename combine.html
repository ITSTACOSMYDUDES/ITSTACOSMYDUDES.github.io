Combine



Combine is a common card that gives the player +100% damage. However, this damage comes at the cost +0.5 seconds of reload time and -2 ammo in your gun often making this card not too usful unless you are quite accurate. This card synergises well with cards such as spray or barrage due to them giving back a large ammount of bullets.
