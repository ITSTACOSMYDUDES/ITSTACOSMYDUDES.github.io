Brawler



Brawler is an uncommon card that grants the player +200% health for 3 seconds upon dealing damage to the oponent. It has no downsides due to it being quite niche and situational for it to actually be useful. This card synergises well with cards such as poison or parasite which deal damage over time, allowing brawler to be constantly active, as well as overpower and pristine preservence allowing the players block to do massive damage.
