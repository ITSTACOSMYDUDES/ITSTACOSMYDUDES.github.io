Scavenger



Scavenger is an uncommon card that completly reloads your gun upon damage being dealt. This comes at the downside of +0.5 seconds of reload time. This card synergises well with cards that make the players damage be dealt over time like poison or parasite as this causes your gun to reload over and over again allowing you to fire endlessly for a short period of time.
