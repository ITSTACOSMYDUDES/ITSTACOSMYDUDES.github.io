Toxic Cloud



Toxic Cloud is a rare card that allows the players bullets to leave behind a damaging gas cloud that scales with damage on whatever they collide with. This comes at the downside of -20% attack speed and +0.5 seconds of reload time. This card synergises with any card that gives bounces or damage such as bouncy or combine as this will allow you to maximise the clouds damage.
