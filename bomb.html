Bombs Away



Bombs Away is an uncommon card that sprays out 6 bombs around you when your block is used. As well as this it gives you a slight boost up above the bombs, allowing it to also serve as a double jump and plus 30% health. The only downside to this card is the +0.25 seconds of block cooldown it gives you. This card synergises well with other cards that give you extra blocks such as echo or shield charge.
