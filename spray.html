Spray



Spray is an uncommon card that allows the players bullets to spray out of thier gun all at once. This is done by giving the player +1000% attack speed and +12 ammo at the cost of -75% damage and +0.25 seconds of reload time. This card synergises well with cards like timed detonation or explosive bullet as these cards allow the player to take full advantage of the spray of their gun.
