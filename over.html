Overpower



Overpower is an uncommon card that gives the player the ability to summon a damaging aura upon blocking. This aura deals 15% of the players max health as damage as well as providing +30% health at the cost of -0.25 seconds of reload speed. This card synergises well with cards that give extra blocks or extra health such as echo or huge.
