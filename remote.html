Remote



Remote is a rare card that allows the players bullet to follow their cursor. This comes with the downsides of -40% bullet speed and +0.25 seconds of reload time. This card synergises with cards that give bounces such as bouncy or mayhem as this gives the player a second chance at hitting their opponent, or homing which allows the players bullets to lock on even better.
