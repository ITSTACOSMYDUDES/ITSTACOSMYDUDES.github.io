Supernova



Supernova is a rare card that causes an explosion around the player a few seconds after blocking. As well as this you also get +50% health at the cost of +0.5 seconds of block cooldown. This card synergises with cards that give extra blocks such as shield charge or echo as this allows the ability to activate multiple times.
