Target Bounce



Target Bounce is an uncommon card that allows the players bullets to lock onto, and bounce directly towards any player insight upon coming into contact with anything. On top of this you also get +1 bounce at the cost of -20% damage and +0.25 seconds of reload time. This card synergises well with cards that give extra bounces such as bouncy or mayhem as this will give the bullets more chances to lock onto any opponents.
