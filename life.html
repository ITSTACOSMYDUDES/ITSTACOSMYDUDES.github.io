Lifestealer



Lifestealer is a rare card that gives the player an aura around them that steals life from opponents inside of it and gives it to you. You also gain +25% health. The aura that lifestealer gives you scales with how much health you have. This card synergises well with any cards that give health such as huge or tank so that your aura is as big as possible.
