Empower



Empower is an uncommon card that allows the player to fire a single charged shot upon blocking. This shot deals extra damage and has extra projectile speed at the downside of +0.25 seconds of block cooldown. However, this is not the main reason to pick this card, the main reason is that upon the charged shot colliding with anything, a block will be activated where the collision occured. This synergises best with any card that grants a block ability such as overpower or bombs away. A unique interaction also occurs when this card is taken with teleport as this will allow the player to teleport to whereever the bullet lands.
