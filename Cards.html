Cards

All of the gameplay inside of Rounds is based around the core mechanic of cards. Cards are items that give special effects to the player based on which one they choose. These come in three different rarity's, being common (clear), uncommon (blue) and rare (purple). These cards effect stats such as bullets and ammo in your gun, the damage the bullets do, the effects the bullets have, the reload time of your gun, the ammount of health you have, the cooldown on your block, the effects your block has, the ammount of times the bullets can bounce, the time between bullets being shot, the splash damage your bullets do, the amount of distance your bullets can pierce through walls, the ammount of speed your bullets have, the distance your bullets can travel, the ammount of life you can steal from your oponents upon hit and the speed of your character.
