Healing Field



Healing field is a common card that spawns a ring on the player that heals everyone inside the rings health after 4 seconds pass. It also gives +30% health at the dowside of +0.25 seconds of block cooldown. This card synergises well with cards that activate a second block such as echo or shield charge.
