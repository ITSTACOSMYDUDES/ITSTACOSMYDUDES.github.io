Tactical Reload



Tactical Reload is a rare card that fully reloads the players gun upon blocking. This comes at the downside of +0.25 seconds of block cooldown. This card synergises well with shields up as this will allow the player to fire thier gun infinitly due to shields up activating a block upon firing the last bullet.
