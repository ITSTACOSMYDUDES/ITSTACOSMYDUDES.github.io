Sneaky



Sneaky is an uncommon card that causes the players bullets to avoid the ground . This comes at the cost of +0.25 seconds of reload time. This card synergises well with cards that give bounces such as bouncy or mayhem as this will allow the bullets to travel the most distance giving you a better chance of hitting your opponent.
