Riccochet



Riccochet is a rare card that causes the players bullets to half in speed after every bounce. You also get +2 bounces and +25% attack speed at the downside of +0.25 seconds of reload time. This card synergises well with cards like explosive bullet or timed detonation as these cards take full adavantage of the bounces.
