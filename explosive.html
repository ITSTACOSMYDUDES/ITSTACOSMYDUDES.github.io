Explosive Bullet



Explosive Bullet is an uncommon card that makes the players bullets explode upon impact. This comes at the downside of -100% attack speed and +0.25 seconds of reload time. This card synergises well with cards that give bullet bounces such as mayhem or bouncy as this allows the players bullets to explode mutiple times.
