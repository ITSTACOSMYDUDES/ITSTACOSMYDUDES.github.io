Echo



Echo is an uncommon card that allows the players block to activate a second time upon using it. On top of this it also grants +30% health at the downside of having an extra +0.25 seconds of block cooldown. This card synergises well with any cards that give the player a block ability such as overpower or bombs away as it will allow the ability to activate a second time, doulbling damage.
