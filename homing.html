Homing



Homing is an uncommon card that allows the players bullets to home an lock onto any player on the map, including yourself. This comes at the cost of -25% damage, -50% attack speed and +0.25 seconds of reload time. This card synergises well with cards that give bounces such as mayhem or target bounce so that the homing has another chance to lock on to the opponent.
