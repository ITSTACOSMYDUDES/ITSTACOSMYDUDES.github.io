Teleport



Teleport is a rare card that allows the player to teleport forwards and even through objects upon blocking. On top of this you also get -30% block cooldown. This card synergises well with cards that grant extra blocks such as echo or shield charge, however it also works well with empower which allows you to teleport to where ever your bullet lands.
