Silence



Silence is an uncommon card that disables all guns and blocks of any opponents within a radius around the player upon blocking. By taking this card you also get +25% health at the downside of +0.25 seconds of reload time. This card synergises well with any cards that give an extra block such as echo or shield charge as this will allow the ability to activate multiple times.
