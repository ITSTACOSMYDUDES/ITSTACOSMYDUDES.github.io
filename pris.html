Pristine Preservence



Pristine Preservence is a rare card that grants the player +400% health for the first 90% of their health. There are no downsides to taking this card as it pairs nicely with any build and is always helpful. This card synergises well with any cards that give extra health and overpower as this will allow you to deal massive damage with one attack.
