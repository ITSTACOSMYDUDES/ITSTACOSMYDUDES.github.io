EMP



EMP is an uncommon card that allows the player to shoot out a ring of slowing projectiles upon blocking. It also grants an extra 30% health at the cost of +0.25 seconds of block cooldown. EMP is an amazing card on its own, being more then capeable of acing your opponent when used right, due to its offensive and defensive capabilities. This card synergises well with cards such as echo and shield charge which make the player block a second time after their first block is used.
