Barrage



Barrage is an uncommon card that makes your gun act like a shotgun. It does this by giving the player extra bullets fired from one shot and extra ammo so multiple shots can be fired. However, this comes at the cost of each bullet doing 70% less dammage and giving your gun +0.25 seconds of reload time. This card synergises well with cards like explosive bullet and timed detonation which benifit from multiple bullets.
