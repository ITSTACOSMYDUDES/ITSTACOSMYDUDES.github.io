Burst



Burst is an common card that makes your gun fire a burst of 3 bullets at a time. As well as giving you a burst of 3 bullets, this card also gives you an extra 3 ammo so a second burst can be fired, at the downside of -60% damage dealt by each bullet and -0.25 seconds of reload speed. This card works well on its own, but also synergises well with cards like poison or combine which give aditional damage whilest not costing you your second shot.
