Thruster



Thruster is an uncommon card that allows the players bullets to leave behind a pushing thruster on whatever they impact with. This comes at the cost of +0.25 seconds of reload time. This card synergises well with any cards that give bounces such as mayhem or bouncy as this will cause your bullets to leave thrusters all over the map.
