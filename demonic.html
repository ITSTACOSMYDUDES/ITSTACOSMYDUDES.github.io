Demonic Pact



Demonic Pact is a rare card that gives a variaty of effects. The first of these is that it completly removes shooting cooldown, meaning that you can fire your gun as fast as you can click. The second effect it grants is bullet splash damage which makes it so when a bullet hits an object, there will be a small damaging radius around it. On top of this, you also get an extra 9 ammo. However, this card comes at the cost of each bullet that is fired costing 10 health, luckily this can't kill you and will only take you down to 1 health before not doing any more damage. It also gives you +0.25 seconds of reload time. This card synergises well with cards such as explosive bullets, timed detonation, or any other card that grants bounces as this will allow you to take the most advantage of the no shooting cooldown.
