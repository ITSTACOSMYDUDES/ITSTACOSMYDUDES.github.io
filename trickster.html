Trickster



Trickster is a rare card that allows the players bullets to increase in damage by 80% with every single bounce. This comes at the downside of -20% damage and +0.5 seconds of reload time. This card synergises well with any card that gives bounces such as bouncy or mayhem as this allows you to stack up your damage fast.
