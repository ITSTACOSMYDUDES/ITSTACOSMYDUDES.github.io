Parasite



Parasite is an uncommon card that deals the players damage to the opponent over the span of 5 seconds upon hit. On top of this it also gives +50 lifesteal, +25% health and +25% damage at the cost of +0.25 seconds of reload time. This card synergises well with cards that activate every time damage is dealt such as scavenger or brawler as this will allow these abilities to activate over and over again.
