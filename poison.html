Poison



Poison is a common card that deals the players damage to the opponent over the span of 3 seconds upon hit. On top of this it also gives the player +70% damage and +30% reload speed at the cost of -1 ammo. This card synergises well with cards that activate every time damage is dealt such as scavenger or brawler as this will allow these abilities to activate over and over again.
