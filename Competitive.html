Competitive

Competitive rounds is a more difficult version of the game created by the comunity as a way for more skilled players to find other players of similar skill levels. The skill of a player is graded by an ELO ranking system with higher ELO meaning you are a better player. Every player starts with 1000 ELO which can be increased by defeating an opponent, however, if you were to lose a game, this would cause your ELO to decrease. The ammount of ELO gained or lost by defeating or losing to an opponent is determined by there ELO. If you defeat a higher ELO opponent then your ELO will increase more upon winning and less upon losing. However, if you lose to a lower ELO opponent then your ELO will decrease more then normal and will increase less upon winning. This is all calculated inside of the Rounds Competitive Discord server.
