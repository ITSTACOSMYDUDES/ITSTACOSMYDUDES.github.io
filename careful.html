Careful Planning



Careful Planning is an uncommon card that gives the player +100% damage. This comes at the cost of -150% attack speed and +0.5 seconds of reload time. While this card seems good at first due to the extra damage, the lower attack speed and extra reload time almost ruin any build. This card is only ever good if you are in desprate need of damage and doesnt really synergise well with any cards.
