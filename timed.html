Timed Detonation



Timed Detonation is a common card that allows the players bullets to leave behind a damaging bomb on whatever they collide with. This comes at the downside of -15% damage and +0.25 seconds of reload time. This card synergises with any card that gives bullet bounces such as bouncy or mayhem as this allows your bullets to leave bombs all over the map.
