Buckshot



Buckshot is an uncommon card that makes your gun act like a shotgun. This card acts very similarly to barrage in the way that it fires out a number of bullets at once. The only difference between the 2 is that buckshot's bullets fire out in a straight direction with force, before fading away after a short distance is traveled, compared to barrage's bullets which act like normal. The upsides to buckshot are that it gives you +5 ammo in your gun and +4 bullets fired every shot, with the downsides being -60% damage dealt by every bullet and +0.25 seconds of reload time. This card synergises well with cards that give extra bullet speed such as quick shot or fastball as this allows the bullets to tavel further before fading away.
